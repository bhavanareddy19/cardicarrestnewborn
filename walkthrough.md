# üìå What Does This Project Do?

**Simple Explanation:** This system acts like a smart medical assistant that looks at a newborn baby's health information and predicts if they might be at risk for cardiac arrest.

**How it works:**
1. **Takes in baby's health data** ‚Üí 10 different health measurements
2. **Processes it through 12 different AI brains** ‚Üí Each "brain" looks at the data differently
3. **Combines all opinions** ‚Üí Like getting a second (or 12th!) opinion from doctors
4. **Gives a risk level** ‚Üí Low, Medium, or High risk

# üìä What Data Does the System Use?

The system analyzes **10 health factors** about newborn babies:

| # | Feature | What It Measures | Danger Levels (High ‚Üí Low Risk) |
|---|---------|-----------------|--------------------------------|
| 1 | **Birth Weight** | How heavy the baby is at birth | WeightTooLow ‚Üí LowWeight ‚Üí NormalWeight |
| 2 | **Family History** | Heart problems in the family | AboveTwoCases ‚Üí ZeroToTwoCases ‚Üí NoCases |
| 3 | **Preterm Birth** | How early the baby was born | 4+ weeks early ‚Üí 2-4 weeks ‚Üí Full term |
